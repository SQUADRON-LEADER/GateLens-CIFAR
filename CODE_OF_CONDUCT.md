# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

This project aims to provide a respectful and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive and respectful.
- Assume positive intent.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on technical improvements.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Offensive comments or exclusionary language.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove harmful comments or contributions and may restrict participation for repeated violations.
